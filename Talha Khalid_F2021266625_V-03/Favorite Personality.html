Favorite Personalities

Hazrat Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم

Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم peace be upon him, is the Last Messenger of Allah (SWT), and the Final/Last Prophet of Islam. He is known as the final Prophet and Messenger according to the Qur'an and Sunnah. He was also a diplomat, merchant, philosopher, orator, legislator, reformer, soldier and general during his lifetime.

Hazrat Abu-Bakr رضي الله عنه

Abu Bakr Abdullah ibn Uthman Abi Quhafa رضي الله عنه was the senior companion and was, through his daughter Aisha, a father-in-law of the Islamic Last Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم, as well as the first Caliph of the Rashidun Caliphate. He is known with the honorific title "Al-Siddiq" by Sunni Muslims.

Shair-E-Ali رضي الله عنه

Hazrat Ali رضي الله عنه was the last Caliph, the successor state to the Islamic Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم political dominions. He is considered by Shia Muslims to be the first Imam, the rightful religious and political successor to Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم.

Hazrat Umar رضي الله عنه

Hazrat Umar رضي الله عنه was the second Rashidun Caliph. He succeeded Abu Bakr رضي الله عنه as the second caliph of the Rashidun Caliphate on 23 August 634. Umar was a senior companion and father-in-law of the Islamic Prophet Muhammad صلى الله عليه وسلم.
